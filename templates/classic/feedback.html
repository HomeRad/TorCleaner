Feedback for WebCleaner

I wrote this software to get rid of advertisings. If you still see advert images on pages you surf on, you are free to send me the url of those pages and I will include filters to remove the adverts.

You can of course always add your own filter rules, they will not be touched on a filter update.

If you have any suggestions or comments about WebCleaner, feel free to email me at calvin@users.sf.net.
